# Sonnet 4.6 Elevated Rate of Errors

Source: https://status.claude.com/incidents/lhws0phdvzz3

## Summary
On September 19, 2024, Claude 3.5 Sonnet experienced an elevated rate of errors due to a service misconfiguration. The incident, officially titled "Sonnet 4.6 Elevated Rate of Errors" but impacting Claude 3.5 Sonnet, lasted for approximately 57 minutes, from 12:49 UTC to 13:46 UTC. Anthropic identified the issue, implemented a fix, and confirmed that services were restored to normal operation.
