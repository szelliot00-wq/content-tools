# Oxygen Content Fusion 9.1 is Now Available!

Source: 

## Summary
Syncro Soft has released Oxygen Content Fusion 9.1, the latest version of its DITA CMS for the AI era, designed to bridge the gap between XML experts and SMEs. This update introduces Oxygen Content Fusion Cloud as a fully managed SaaS platform, alongside significant enhancements to the Enterprise version. Key improvements include advanced AI capabilities, streamlined collaboration tools, a centralized review task dashboard, and an enhanced visual editing experience.

## Key takeaways
- **Oxygen Content Fusion 9.1 Release:** Syncro Soft has launched Oxygen Content Fusion Enterprise 9.1 and introduced Oxygen Content Fusion Cloud, a new fully managed SaaS platform for collaborative review, content management, and automated publishing.
- **Advanced AI Capabilities:** The update features an enhanced Oxygen AI Positron plugin with a "Planner AI Agent" capable of orchestrating complex, project-wide operations (e.g., bulk translations, updates), benefiting from smarter context awareness and supporting vision prompts.
- **Streamlined Collaboration & Review:** New @mentions facilitate direct communication and instant notifications within the review process, complemented by a Centralized Review Task Dashboard for at-a-glance tracking of task progress, due dates, and reviewer statuses.
- **Enhanced Authoring Experience:** Authors benefit from an Advanced Visual Editor with "sticky" DITA table headers and DITAVAL flagging, alongside new project carousel starter templates for quicker onboarding and refined user interfaces.
- **Specialized Industry Support:** Version 9.1 introduces built-in support for S1000D® issue 4.x, offering schema-guided editing and specialized visual authoring styles for the aerospace and defense sectors.
