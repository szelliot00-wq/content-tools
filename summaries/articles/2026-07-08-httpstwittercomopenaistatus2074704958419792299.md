# GPT-5.6 Sol, along with Terra and Luna, will launch publicly this Thursday

Source: https://twitter.com/OpenAI/status/2074704958419792299

## Summary
OpenAI announced via their official X (Twitter) account that GPT-5.6 Sol, along with two additional models named Terra and Luna, will launch publicly on Thursday, July 10, 2026. The post was made on July 8, 2026 and quickly gained significant traction, accumulating over 2.1 million views, 24,000+ likes, and 4,100+ reposts within hours of posting.
