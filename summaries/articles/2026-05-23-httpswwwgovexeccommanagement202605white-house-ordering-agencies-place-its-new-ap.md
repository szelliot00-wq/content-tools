# White House ordering agencies to place its new app on all employees' govt phones

Source: https://www.govexec.com/management/2026/05/white-house-ordering-agencies-place-its-new-app-all-employees-government-phones/413738/

## Summary
The White House is directing federal agencies to install its newly created official app on all government-issued employee phones. The app features official statements, policy announcements, and a feed of social media posts from White House accounts and the president. IT and cybersecurity experts have characterized the move as placing the Trump administration in unprecedented and "dangerous" territory.

## Key takeaways
- The White House has ordered agencies to pre-install its app on all federal employees' government phones.
- The app serves as a direct communication channel, pushing official statements, policy announcements, and presidential social media content to federal workers.
