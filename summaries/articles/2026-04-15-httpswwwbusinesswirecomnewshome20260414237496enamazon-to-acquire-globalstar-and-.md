# Amazon to acquire Globalstar and expand Amazon Leo satellite network

Source: https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260414237496/en/Amazon-to-Acquire-Globalstar-and-Expand-Amazon-Leo-Satellite-Network

## Summary
Amazon has announced its intention to acquire Globalstar, a strategic move aimed at significantly expanding its Amazon Leo satellite network. This acquisition underscores Amazon's growing commitment to the competitive satellite internet market. By integrating Globalstar's assets, Amazon seeks to bolster its infrastructure and accelerate the delivery of global connectivity services.

## Key takeaways
- Amazon is making a major strategic acquisition by purchasing Globalstar.
- The primary purpose of this acquisition is to expand and enhance Amazon's Leo satellite network.
- This move signifies Amazon's serious intent to compete and grow its presence in the global satellite internet and communication industry.
- The acquisition will likely involve integrating Globalstar's existing satellite infrastructure, spectrum licenses, or technology to improve the reach and capability of Amazon's connectivity services.
