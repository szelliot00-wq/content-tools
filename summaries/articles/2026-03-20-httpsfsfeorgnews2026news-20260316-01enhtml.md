# FSFE supporters affected: Payment provider Nexi cancelled us

Source: https://fsfe.org/news/2026/news-20260316-01.en.html

## Summary
The Free Software Foundation Europe (FSFE) announced that its payment provider, Nexi, abruptly terminated their long-standing contract without clear justification, impacting their ability to process donations from supporters. This cancellation, which came with only 24 hours' notice, forced the FSFE to scramble to find alternative payment solutions and has disrupted their financial operations. The organization suspects the termination might be politically motivated or due to compliance issues with financial regulations, highlighting broader challenges faced by non-profits in the digital payment landscape.

## Key takeaways
- **Abrupt Termination of Services:** Nexi, a major payment provider, cancelled FSFE's contract with only 24 hours' notice, severely disrupting their financial operations.
- **Lack of Clear Justification:** Nexi provided no specific reason for the termination beyond vague references to "internal policy" or "risk assessment," leaving FSFE to speculate on the cause, including potential political motives or compliance issues related to financial regulations (e.g., anti-money laundering, counter-terrorism financing).
- **Impact on Non-Profits:** The incident underscores the vulnerability of non-profit organizations to sudden service cancellations by financial institutions, which can cripple their ability to raise funds and operate.
- **Importance of Diversification:** This event highlights the critical need for organizations, especially non-profits, to diversify their payment processing options and not rely solely on a single provider to mitigate such risks.
- **Challenges in the Digital Payment Landscape:** The experience reflects broader challenges in the digital payment ecosystem, where payment providers often operate with limited transparency and can make unilateral decisions impacting their clients, especially those in advocacy or non-mainstream sectors.
- **Call for Action/Support:** FSFE is actively seeking new payment providers and encourages supporters to switch to alternative donation methods while they resolve the issue.
