# Apple Says Mac Studio and Mac Mini Will Be in Short Supply for Months

Source: https://www.macrumors.com/2026/04/30/mac-studio-mac-mini-constrained-months/

## Summary
Apple has announced that both the Mac Studio and Mac Mini will be in short supply for several months. The supply constraints suggest that demand for these machines is outpacing Apple's current production capacity. The article, shared on Hacker News, generated some community discussion around the news.

## Key takeaways
- Apple has officially acknowledged supply shortages for the Mac Studio and Mac Mini models.
- The shortages are expected to last for several months, meaning customers may face delays or difficulty purchasing these machines.
- The constrained supply could be driven by high consumer demand, manufacturing limitations, or component shortages.
- Customers looking to purchase either device should anticipate potential wait times or limited availability.
