# Italy, Belgium set to lose gas supply after biggest LNG plant bombed

Source: https://www.politico.eu/article/italy-belgium-lose-gas-supply-world-biggest-lng-plant-bombed/

## Summary
The world's largest liquefied natural gas (LNG) plant has been bombed, leading to significant disruptions in global gas supply. As a direct consequence, Italy and Belgium are reportedly set to lose their gas provisions. This incident underscores the extreme vulnerability of critical energy infrastructure and poses a substantial threat to Europe's energy security.

## Key takeaways
- The largest LNG plant globally has been subjected to a bombing.
- Italy and Belgium are expected to lose their gas supply due to this attack.
- The incident highlights the critical vulnerability of major energy infrastructure worldwide.
- This event will have significant immediate and potential long-term implications for European energy security.
