# NetNewsWire Status

Source: https://inessential.com/2026/06/15/netnewswire-status.html

## Summary
The article is a June 2026 status update on NetNewsWire, an RSS reader app, written by its developer one year into retirement. The developer spent the year addressing technical debt, modernizing the codebase, and fixing bugs rather than adding new features, with 2,188 commits made in the past year. The foundation work is now paying off, with new features beginning to ship.

## Key takeaways
- The developer retired on June 6, 2025, and has dedicated retirement time to NetNewsWire development.
- The past year focused on modernization, tech debt reduction, and bug fixes — foundational work before new features could be added.
- 2,188 commits were made over the year, reflecting significant development activity.
- New features are beginning to ship (e.g., iCloud Storage Stats, Error Log) with more in beta (Dinosaurs, Current Activity, Activity Log, Account Stats).
- The project involves other contributors, with Stuart Breckenridge noted specifically.
- A Discourse forum exists for community discussion.
- The developer also references a separate RSS reader project called Gobbler.
