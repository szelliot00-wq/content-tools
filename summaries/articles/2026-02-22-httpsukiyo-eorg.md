# Japanese Woodblock Print Search

Source: https://ukiyo-e.org/

## Summary
The "Japanese Woodblock Print Search" refers to ukiyo-e.org, an extensive online database dedicated to Japanese woodblock prints. This platform aggregates images and metadata from various museums, collections, and institutions worldwide into a single, searchable resource. It serves as a centralized hub for enthusiasts, researchers, and the public to explore, identify, and learn about ukiyo-e art.

## Key takeaways
-   **Centralized Ukiyo-e Database:** Ukiyo-e.org provides a comprehensive, single point of access for Japanese woodblock prints.
-   **Aggregates Global Collections:** It compiles data and images from numerous diverse collections across the globe.
-   **Advanced Search Capabilities:** The platform offers powerful search tools to help users identify, study, and cross-reference prints.
-   **Resource for Research and Appreciation:** It significantly aids scholars, art historians, and enthusiasts in researching and appreciating ukiyo-e art.
-   **Promotes Accessibility:** By digitizing and centralizing information, it makes a vast array of prints more accessible to a global audience.
