# Ask HN: What do you look for in your first 10 hires?

Source: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47435782

## Summary
The article is an "Ask HN" post where the author, who assists companies in recruiting founding engineers, seeks advice from the Hacker News community. Having developed a basic understanding of what to look for, the author specifically asks experienced hirers what non-technical qualities they prioritize in a company's first 10 hires, beyond their technical abilities. The aim is to gather diverse insights into successful early-stage hiring strategies.

## Key takeaways
- Beyond technical expertise, non-technical attributes are crucial for early-stage hires, particularly founding engineers.
- Key qualities to seek include adaptability, proactive problem-solving, and a strong sense of ownership.
- Cultural fit and effective communication skills are paramount for success in a small, rapidly growing team.
