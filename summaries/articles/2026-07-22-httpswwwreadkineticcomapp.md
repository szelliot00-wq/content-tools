# Show HN: ReadKinetic – a free, local-first speed reader for your own books

Source: https://www.readkinetic.com/app/

## Summary
ReadKinetic is a free, local-first speed reading application that lets users import and read their own books. It presents a RSVP-style (Rapid Serial Visual Presentation) reader interface where words are displayed one at a time at a user-configured words-per-minute rate. The app includes library management, bookmarks, reading stats and streaks, and a scrubbing control for navigation.

## Key takeaways
- Speed reading is configurable starting at 300 WPM, with a setup flow that lets users find their preferred pace before diving in.
- Local-first design means books and reading data stay on the user's device rather than in the cloud.
- Navigation is gesture-driven: hold to read, left/right to scrub through content.
- Includes reading progress tracking with stats and streaks to encourage consistent reading habits.
- Free to use with an import feature to bring in your own books.
