## Summary
A Component Content Management System (CCMS) manages content at the component level rather than the document level. The article, published by Fluid Topics, covers the features, benefits, and limitations of a CCMS, and explains how CCMSs and Product Knowledge Platforms work as complementary tools.

## Key takeaways
- A CCMS operates at the **component level**, not the document level, allowing more granular content management.
- CCMSs have distinct **features, benefits, and limitations** worth evaluating before adoption.
- CCMSs are positioned as **complementary** to Product Knowledge Platforms, not replacements.
