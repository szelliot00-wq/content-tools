## Summary
Written by Eden DaSilva and published July 15, 2026 on Pigeons & Planes, the article explores the culture and nostalgia surrounding music piracy through the lens of Rob Sheridan, former creative director of Nine Inch Nails and an outspoken piracy advocate. Sheridan traces his connection to early internet music sharing back to 1997, when he downloaded a leaked Nine Inch Nails single in RealAudio format. The piece centers on the now-defunct music torrent communities What.CD and Oink, examining what made that era of music discovery feel meaningful compared to today's streaming landscape.
