# RamAIn (YC W26) Is Hiring

Source: https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/ramain/jobs/hqvmyKN-founding-gtm-engineer

## Summary
RamAIn, a YC W26 startup (founded 2025), builds fast computer-use AI agents that automate enterprise workflows by operating legacy systems, desktop apps, and web portals like a human — but 10x faster. The company is hiring its first business hire: a Founding GTM Engineer based in San Francisco, offering $120K–$180K salary plus 0.30%–1.20% equity.

## Key takeaways
- **Role:** Founding GTM Engineer (Sales) — full-time, SF-based, US work authorization required, 1+ years experience
- **Compensation:** $120K–$180K salary + 0.30%–1.20% equity stake
- **Responsibilities:** Building outbound infrastructure from scratch — lead enrichment, ICP scoring, AI-personalized sequences, CRM architecture, and pipeline automation
- **Requirements:** Hands-on experience with GTM tooling (Clay, Apollo, Instantly), Python/SQL proficiency, and genuine interest in applying LLMs to GTM workflows
- **Founders:** Shourya Vir Jain (CEO, ex-McKinsey, IIT Delhi) and Vansh Ramani (CTO, CMU AI researcher, contributor to Meta's FAISS vector search)
- **Company stage:** Very early — team of 4, this hire would be the third full-time employee working directly with founders
