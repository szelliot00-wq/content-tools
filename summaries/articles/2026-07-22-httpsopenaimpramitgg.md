# Show HN: A new kind of FPS aim trainer

Source: https://openaim.pramit.gg/

## Summary
OpenAim is a first-person shooter aim trainer that distinguishes itself by building personalized drills from a measured model of the user's aim. It requires a brief two-minute calibration process to capture two physical constants: mouse DPI (counts per inch) and in-game sensitivity (counts to degrees). All data and configuration remain local to the user's machine.

## Key takeaways
- OpenAim personalizes aim drills based on a physical model of your specific mouse and sensitivity setup, rather than using generic defaults.
- Calibration requires two inputs: mouse CPI/DPI and the game's sensitivity mapping (counts to degrees).
- The calibration process takes approximately two minutes and can be skipped in favor of default values.
- All data stays on the local machine — no cloud accounts or data sharing required.
