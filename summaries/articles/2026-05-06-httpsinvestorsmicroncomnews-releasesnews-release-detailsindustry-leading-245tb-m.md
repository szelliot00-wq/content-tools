# 245TB Micron 6600 ION Data Center SSD Now Shipping

Source: https://investors.micron.com/news-releases/news-release-details/industry-leading-245tb-micron-6600-ion-data-center-ssd-now

## Summary
Micron has announced that its 6600 ION Data Center SSD, featuring an industry-leading 245TB storage capacity, is now shipping. This marks a significant milestone in enterprise storage density, targeting data center workloads that demand massive capacity in a single drive. The announcement generated discussion on Hacker News, reflecting notable interest from the tech community.

## Key takeaways
- Micron's 6600 ION SSD offers an industry-leading 245TB of storage in a single data center drive
- The drive is now actively shipping, moving from announcement to commercial availability
- It is specifically designed for data center environments and high-demand enterprise workloads
- The product represents a significant leap in SSD storage density, potentially reducing the number of drives needed in large-scale deployments
- The announcement garnered attention in the tech community, with 39 points and 29 comments on Hacker News
