# Vibe coding my way to a healthy family: Introducing Gamow Labs

Source: https://www.ddmckinnon.com/2026/06/09/vibe-coding-my-way-to-a-healthy-family-introducing-gamow-labs/

## Summary
Daniel D. McKinnon recounts the tragic death of his first son Owen due to a missed genetic diagnosis, and how that experience drove him to action when his second son Warren was born. Using AI-assisted ("vibe") coding, he rapidly built a genetic analysis prototype that not only confirmed Warren's health but also identified the exact mutation that had killed Owen — something top sequencing labs had missed. This experience led him to found Gamow Labs, a venture aimed at improving genetic analysis and diagnosis.

## Key takeaways
- The author lost his first son Owen to what appears to have been a preventable missed genetic diagnosis.
- When his second son Warren was born, he took matters into his own hands and built a rapid prototype using AI-assisted coding to analyze genetic data.
- His prototype outperformed professional sequencing labs by identifying Owen's fatal genetic mutation — raising serious questions about the state of clinical genetic testing.
- The experience directly inspired the creation of Gamow Labs, presumably a company or research effort focused on better genetic diagnostics.
- The article is a personal origin story framing "vibe coding" (low-friction, AI-augmented development) as a powerful tool for solving real-world, high-stakes problems.
