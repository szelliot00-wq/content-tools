# Kagi Translate now supports LinkedIn Speak as an output language

Source: https://translate.kagi.com/?from=en&to=LinkedIn+speak

## Summary
Kagi Translate has launched a new, satirical output language called "LinkedIn Speak," allowing users to transform text into the often-clichéd jargon found on the professional networking platform. This unique feature is accessible directly through the Kagi Translate website. The announcement generated significant community interest and discussion, evidenced by its high engagement on Hacker News.

## Key takeaways
- Kagi Translate is expanding its language options with a humorous and satirical "LinkedIn Speak" feature.
- This new output language aims to parody the characteristic jargon and clichés common in professional networking.
- The strong positive reception and high engagement on Hacker News suggest a significant interest in innovative, and even humorous, tech features within the developer community.
