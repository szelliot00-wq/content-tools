# OpenAI to confidentially file for IPO as soon as Friday

Source: https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/20/openai-ipo-filing.html

## Summary
OpenAI is preparing to confidentially file its IPO prospectus as soon as Friday, working with Goldman Sachs and Morgan Stanley as lead banks. The company, valued at over $850 billion by private investors, has been targeting a Q4 public debut. This comes amid intensifying rivalry with Elon Musk's SpaceX/xAI (which is also filing its IPO) and Anthropic, which is pursuing a $900 billion valuation.

## Key takeaways
- OpenAI plans to confidentially file its IPO draft prospectus imminently, potentially as soon as Friday.
- Goldman Sachs and Morgan Stanley are the lead underwriters on the deal.
- OpenAI is valued at over $850 billion privately and has raised more than $180 billion from investors, but continues to burn cash at a historic rate.
- CEO Sam Altman faces pressure from investors to demonstrate financial viability while competing against rivals like Anthropic and SpaceX/xAI.
- Elon Musk's SpaceX (merged with xAI) is simultaneously filing its own IPO prospectus, escalating the public market rivalry between Musk and Altman.
- Anthropic is in talks to raise at a $900 billion valuation — potentially surpassing OpenAI — and reported $30 billion in annualized revenue as of April.
- A recent court ruling went against Musk in his lawsuit alleging OpenAI broke its nonprofit mission, with an advisory jury finding he sued too late.
