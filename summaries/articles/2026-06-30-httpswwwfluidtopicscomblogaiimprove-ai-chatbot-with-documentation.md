# Your AI Chatbot Is Only as Good as Your Documentation

Source: https://www.fluidtopics.com/blog/ai/improve-ai-chatbot-with-documentation/

## Summary
This article from Fluid Topics argues that AI chatbot performance is directly tied to the quality of the documentation powering it. It outlines 5 core limitations of AI chatbots and then provides 7 actionable strategies teams can use to improve chatbot outcomes by investing in better documentation practices.

## Key takeaways
- AI chatbots are only as effective as the documentation they are trained on or retrieve from.
- There are 5 key limitations of AI chatbots that teams should understand before deploying them for self-service.
- Improving documentation quality is a primary lever for improving chatbot relevance and accuracy.
- Teams across content, product, and support have a shared responsibility in optimizing documentation for AI use.
- There are 7 concrete ways organizations can enhance chatbot outcomes through better documentation practices.
