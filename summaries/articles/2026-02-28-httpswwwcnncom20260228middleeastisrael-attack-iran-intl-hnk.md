# Israel launches strike against Iran, declares state of emergency across country

Source: https://www.cnn.com/2026/02/28/middleeast/israel-attack-iran-intl-hnk

## Summary
Israel has launched a military strike against Iran, marking a significant escalation in regional tensions. Following this action, Israel concurrently declared a nationwide state of emergency across its own territory. This development indicates a heightened state of conflict and alert between the two nations.

## Key takeaways
- Israel has initiated direct military action by launching a strike against Iran.
- A state of emergency has been declared across Israel, indicating heightened internal alert and mobilization.
- These events signify a major escalation in the ongoing conflict between Israel and Iran.
