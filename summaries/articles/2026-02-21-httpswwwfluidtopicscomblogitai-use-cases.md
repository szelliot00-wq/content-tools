# 7 AI Use Cases Every CIO Should Prioritize

Source: https://www.fluidtopics.com/blog/it/ai-use-cases/

## Summary
The article, "7 AI Use Cases Every CIO Should Prioritize," guides Chief Information Officers on selecting impactful AI applications that deliver measurable return on investment. It emphasizes a shift from technology deployment to business outcomes, outlining seven specific AI use cases that improve productivity, reduce costs, and accelerate growth. Each use case details its strategic objective and key benefits, offering CIOs a roadmap for implementing value-driven AI initiatives this year.

## Key takeaways
- CIOs must prioritize AI applications based on measurable business outcomes and ROI, rather than just tech deployment, due to increasing pressure on productivity and cost reduction.
- Seven high-priority AI use cases are identified: AI-Powered Self-Service Support, Augmented Support Agents, RFI/RFP Responses & Commercial Proposals, SDR Agent for Sales Prospecting, Employee Onboarding, Product Innovation R&D, and Coding Copilots for Software Development.
- These use cases collectively aim to enhance customer satisfaction, boost employee productivity, accelerate revenue generation, shorten time-to-market for products, and improve software development efficiency and quality.
- Successful AI implementation should target processes that create bottlenecks, involve repetitive and error-prone human tasks, or hamper quality, costs, maintainability, or productivity.
- CIOs should select AI projects that are smooth to implement and offer easily measurable results to demonstrate value quickly and effectively.
