# Jeff Bezos wants Washington Post’s newsroom budget halved, productivity doubled

Source: https://www.nytimes.com/2026/03/14/business/media/washington-post-jeff-bezos-layoffs.html

## Summary
Jeff Bezos, owner of the Washington Post, is reportedly pushing for a radical overhaul of the newspaper's newsroom operations. He aims to halve the newsroom's budget while simultaneously demanding a doubling of its productivity. This move signals a significant restructuring effort, likely involving widespread layoffs, to improve the Post's financial efficiency and output.

## Key takeaways
- Jeff Bezos is demanding a 50% reduction in the Washington Post's newsroom budget.
- Concurrently, he expects the newsroom's productivity to double, implying a much higher output with fewer resources.
- This initiative points to a major restructuring of the Washington Post, likely leading to significant layoffs and changes in its operational model.
- The goal appears to be enhancing financial efficiency and sustainability for the publication, which could have a substantial impact on the scope and quality of its journalism.
