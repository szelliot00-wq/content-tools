# FCC exempts Netgear from ban on foreign routers, doesn't explain why

Source: https://arstechnica.com/tech-policy/2026/04/fcc-exempts-netgear-from-ban-on-foreign-routers-doesnt-explain-why/

## Summary
The FCC has issued a controversial decision to exempt Netgear from its ban on certain foreign-made routers. Notably, the commission did not provide any public explanation for why Netgear was granted this specific exception. This lack of transparency regarding the exemption criteria raises questions about the consistency and justification of the FCC's enforcement policies for the ban.

## Key takeaways
- The FCC has granted an exemption to Netgear, allowing it to bypass the ban on certain foreign-made routers.
- The commission did not offer any public explanation for its decision to exempt Netgear.
- This lack of transparency regarding the exemption process is a point of concern.
- The incident underscores the ongoing existence and selective enforcement of the FCC's ban on particular foreign routers.
