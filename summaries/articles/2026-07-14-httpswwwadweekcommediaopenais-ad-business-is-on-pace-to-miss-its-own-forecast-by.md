# OpenAI's Ad Business Is on Pace to Miss Its Own Forecast by 90%, Analyst Says

Source: https://www.adweek.com/media/openais-ad-business-is-on-pace-to-miss-its-own-forecast-by-90-analyst-says/

## Summary
OpenAI projected $2.5 billion in ad revenue for 2025 and $100 billion by 2030, but analyst firm Emarketer forecasts the entire U.S. chatbot ad market will generate less than $1 billion this year and only $5.41 billion by 2030. This puts OpenAI on pace to miss its own five-year forecast by roughly 90%. OpenAI's ad trial began in February 2025, and its projections rest on a series of optimistic assumptions that analysts consider unrealistic.

## Key takeaways
- OpenAI's $100 billion ad revenue forecast by 2030 dwarfs Emarketer's $5.41 billion ceiling for the entire U.S. chatbot ad market.
- The whole standalone chatbot ad category (ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, Google AI Mode, Amazon Alexa for Shopping) is expected to generate under $1 billion in 2025.
- OpenAI's projections assume it will capture search ad budgets en masse, dominate a mature chatbot ad market, and outperform every historical ad format simultaneously.
- OpenAI only began its ad trial in February 2025, making the scale of its revenue forecasts especially aggressive given the early stage of the business.
