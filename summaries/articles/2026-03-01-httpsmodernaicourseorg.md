# 10-202: Introduction to Modern AI (CMU)

Source: https://modernaicourse.org

## Summary
The "10-202: Introduction to Modern AI" is a rigorous introductory course offered at Carnegie Mellon University, focusing on the latest advancements in artificial intelligence. Taught by Aditi Raghunathan and Zico Kolter, it covers fundamental concepts and state-of-the-art models such as deep learning, large language models (LLMs), diffusion models, and reinforcement learning. The course aims to provide students with a strong theoretical and practical foundation in modern AI, including its societal implications, preparing them for further engagement with the field.

## Key takeaways
- The course offers a rigorous introduction specifically to *modern* AI, emphasizing recent advancements like deep learning, large language models (LLMs), and diffusion models over traditional AI paradigms.
- It covers a broad range of contemporary AI topics, including core deep learning concepts, generative models, and reinforcement learning.
- Students gain both conceptual understanding and practical skills, preparing them for hands-on work and further study in AI.
- The curriculum also incorporates discussions on the ethical and societal implications of modern AI technologies.
