# What is Tacit Knowledge?

Source: https://www.fluidtopics.com/blog/content-ops/what-is-tacit-knowledge/

## Summary
This article serves as a comprehensive guide to understanding and leveraging tacit knowledge within an organization. It aims to define tacit knowledge, outline its common characteristics, provide concrete examples, and detail effective methods for capturing it. Furthermore, the post clarifies the distinctions between tacit and explicit knowledge to help companies improve their institutional knowledge.

## Key takeaways
- The article will define tacit knowledge and describe its common characteristics.
- It will offer concrete examples of tacit knowledge and explain practical ways to capture it.
- The content will compare tacit and explicit knowledge to highlight their fundamental differences.
- The ultimate goal is to equip companies with the understanding and tools to upgrade and effectively utilize their institutional tacit knowledge.
