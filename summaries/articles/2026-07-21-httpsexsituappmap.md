## Summary

Ex Situ is an open-source, map-based tool for cataloguing cultural artifacts that have been displaced from their original geographic locations — objects now held in museums, collections, or institutions far from where they originated. The interface centers on an interactive world map (built on Protomaps and OpenStreetMap) with a browsable sidebar organized into Places and Collections. Users can search, filter, and export data about these displaced objects.
