# Spirit Airlines Is Winding Down All Operations

Source: https://www.spiritrestructuring.com/

## Summary
Spirit Airlines has announced that it is winding down all of its operations. The airline, which had previously filed for bankruptcy, is now ceasing business entirely rather than restructuring or being acquired. This marks the end of one of the United States' largest ultra-low-cost carriers.

## Key takeaways
- Spirit Airlines is fully shutting down all operations, not restructuring or reorganizing.
- The airline has set up a dedicated restructuring website (spiritrestructuring.com) to communicate updates related to the wind-down process.
- This represents a significant development in the U.S. budget airline industry, as Spirit was one of the largest low-cost carriers.
- Customers, employees, and creditors will likely be impacted by the complete cessation of operations.
