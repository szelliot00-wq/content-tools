# Over 80% of 16 to 24-year-olds would vote to rejoin the EU

Source: https://www.itv.com/news/2026-02-19/over-80-of-16-to-24-year-olds-would-vote-to-rejoin-the-eu-itv-poll-finds

## Summary
An ITV poll has revealed that a substantial majority of young people in the UK, specifically over 80% of those aged 16 to 24, would vote to rejoin the European Union. This finding highlights a strong pro-EU sentiment within the younger demographic regarding the UK's relationship with the EU.

## Key takeaways
- Over 80% of 16 to 24-year-olds would vote to rejoin the EU, indicating a very strong pro-EU sentiment among this younger demographic.
- The poll, conducted by ITV, points to a potential significant generational divide in attitudes towards the UK's membership in the European Union.
- This high level of support among young people could have long-term political implications as this cohort ages and gains more electoral influence.
