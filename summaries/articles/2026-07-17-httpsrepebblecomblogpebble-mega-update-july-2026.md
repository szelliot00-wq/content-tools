# Pebble Mega Update – July 2026

Source: https://repebble.com/blog/pebble-mega-update-july-2026

## Summary
The July 2026 Pebble mega update covers shipping progress on the Pebble Time 2, major software improvements across battery life, the SDK, and stability, and production updates for the Pebble Round 2 and Index 01. Over 23,000 PT2 watches have been built, with remaining pre-orders expected to ship by end of July. The software team has shipped significant open-source updates including new APIs, extended battery life, and community-driven features.

## Key takeaways
- Pebble Time 2 is 80%+ through pre-order fulfillment; all remaining colors ship by July 28–31, after which watches will briefly be in-stock with no wait at rePebble.com.
- PT2 median battery life has improved to ~21 days; Pebble 2 Duo hit 30+ days, up from 17 days last summer.
- New SDK APIs were released: Touch Screen, Speaker, and RGB Backlight, enabling richer third-party apps — 2,120 apps and watchfaces already exist for the new hardware.
- Index 01 software is feature-complete in the Pebble app, supporting iOS Reminders, Obsidian, Google Tasks, Calendar, MCPs, webhooks, and optional encrypted cloud backup.
- A complex Bluetooth architecture change (reversing PPoGATT) is underway, required to enable iOS notification replies via AccessorySetupKit — it will take time as it requires a firmware update across all watches in the field.
- Community developers have contributed Apple HealthKit/Google Health sync, notification filtering, new language packs, and upcoming features like HRV, SpO2, and a mic API.
- Pebble Round 2 and Index 01 are both in production with updates and timelines shared; Index 01 pre-orderers have an important note about a design change (foam adhesive and clip).
