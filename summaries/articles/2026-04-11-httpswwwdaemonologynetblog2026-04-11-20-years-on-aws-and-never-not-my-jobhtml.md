# 20 Years on AWS and Never Not My Job

Source: https://www.daemonology.net/blog/2026-04-11-20-years-on-AWS-and-never-not-my-job.html

## Summary
The article reflects on the author's remarkable 20-year journey with Amazon Web Services, starting from its very early days with S3 and EC2. It highlights how AWS has been a constant and ever-evolving presence in their professional life, demanding continuous learning and adaptation. The author emphasizes that despite two decades, the platform's rapid innovation means there has always been something new to master, making it "never not their job" to keep up.

## Key takeaways
- **Constant Evolution Requires Continuous Learning:** Cloud platforms like AWS evolve so rapidly that staying proficient demands a perpetual commitment to learning new services and updates.
- **Early Adoption Pays Off:** Engaging with foundational technologies from their inception can lead to a long and relevant career path in that domain.
- **AWS's Pervasive Impact:** Cloud computing, exemplified by AWS, has become a deeply integrated and essential component of modern IT professional roles, shaping careers over decades.
- **The "Always On" Nature of Cloud Careers:** A career deeply tied to a dynamic platform like AWS often means the work of understanding, adapting, and innovating is continuous and never truly finished.
