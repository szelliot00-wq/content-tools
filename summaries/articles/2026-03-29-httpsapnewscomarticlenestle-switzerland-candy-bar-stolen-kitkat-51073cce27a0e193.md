# Nestlé says 413,793 KitKat candy bars stolen en route from Italy to Poland

Source: https://apnews.com/article/nestle-switzerland-candy-bar-stolen-kitkat-51073cce27a0e193651aa7f31aaa506e

## Summary
Nestlé has announced that 413,793 KitKat candy bars, valued at approximately $550,000 (500,000 Swiss francs), were stolen in transit. The theft occurred sometime between September 26 and October 10 while the chocolate bars were being transported from Italy to Poland. Nestlé expressed regret over the incident and is working to ensure its customers receive their orders promptly.

## Key takeaways
-   A shipment containing 413,793 KitKat candy bars was stolen en route from Italy to Poland.
-   The value of the stolen candy bars is estimated to be around $550,000.
-   The theft took place over a two-week period, between September 26 and October 10.
-   Nestlé, the manufacturer, stated they regret the incident and are working to ensure customers are served as quickly as possible despite the loss.
