# Fluidtopics — Changes 2026-06-22

### Platform

## What Changed

The Fluid Topics homepage was significantly stripped down — a large middle section was removed, eliminating several feature showcases and social proof elements that previously appeared on the page.

## Notable Updates

- **AI feature sections removed from homepage**: Dedicated blocks for "AI Search," "Generative AI," and "Agentic AI" (which was previously badged "NEW") are no longer displayed on the main page — though these products still appear in the footer/nav product links.
- **Connectors & Integrations showcase removed**: The scrolling logo wall listing 100+ integrations (MadCap, Adobe, GitHub, Salesforce, Zendesk, OpenAI, etc.) is gone from the homepage.
- **Customer story removed**: The Johnson Controls case study (featuring Nelson Abbey's quote about 4,000+ DITA documents across 25 portals) and the "See all customer stories" CTA were removed.
- **Enterprise/security section removed**: Blocks covering Security Controls, 99.9%+ uptime SLA, Compliance & Governance, and Scalability are no longer on the homepage.
- **Primary CTA removed**: The "Take the Next Step with Fluid Topics" / "Get in touch" section at the bottom of the main content is gone.

The page appears to have been condensed to a leaner layout, likely moving detailed feature and trust content to dedicated subpages rather than the homepage.
