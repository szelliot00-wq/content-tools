# Tridion — Changes 2026-05-04

### Product overview

## What Changed
The Tridion homepage has been significantly expanded with a new promotional alert banner and a much more structured, detailed page layout, though the core product messaging and content remain largely the same.

- **New report promotion**: A banner alert now highlights a new report titled "Content Unlocked: What enterprise AI doesn't know it's missing," with a direct "Get the report" link — suggesting a new thought leadership/content marketing asset.
- **New footer product listings**: The footer now explicitly lists individual products including **Tridion Docs**, **Tridion Sites**, **Semantic AI**, **Cloud**, and **Fonto XML Editor**, making the product portfolio more visible.
- **New solution categories listed**: Footer reveals solution focus areas including **Technical Communication**, **Field Service**, **Customer Support**, **Digital Experiences**, and **SMB Website Management**.

### Sites editions

## What Changed
The Tridion Sites page was significantly rebuilt with a richer, more structured layout and interactive elements, though the core product content and pricing tiers remain largely the same. The most meaningful new additions are a promotional news banner, an embedded contact/lead capture form, and clarified feature availability per tier.

## Notable Updates
- **New promotional banner**: Highlights a new report titled "Content Unlocked: What enterprise AI doesn't know it's missing" with a "Get the report" CTA.
- **Embedded contact form added**: A full lead capture form (First Name, Last Name, Business Email, Company Name, Job Title, Phone, Country/Region, Additional Message) now appears directly on the page, replacing or supplementing the previous "Contact" button-only approach.
- **Feature availability clarified in comparison table**: The new table explicitly shows Yes/No values per tier, revealing that "Add and manage page regions," "Instant Site," and "Runs on sustainable energy" are now clearly marked as **Enterprise-only** features, while Essential and Team show "No."
- **Semantic AI feature**: Previously shown as available only "On-demand," it is now explicitly marked **Yes for Enterprise** and **No for Essential/Team**.
- **Personalization and A/B testing**: Remains "On-demand" for Enterprise but is now explicitly **No** for Essential and Team tiers.
- **Cookie consent dialog**: A new cookie notice dialog (with Accept All, Reject All, and Settings options) has been added to the page.
