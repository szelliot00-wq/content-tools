### Platform

## What Changed
A large portion of the homepage content was removed — the "Designed for AI, Built for the Future" feature section and several supporting sections below it were stripped out, significantly shortening the page.

## Notable Updates
- **AI feature cards removed**: The individual homepage sections for **AI Search**, **Generative AI**, and **Agentic AI** (previously labeled "NEW") no longer appear as standalone content blocks on the homepage.
- **Connectors & Integrations section removed**: The showcase of 100+ integrations (MadCap, Adobe, GitHub, Salesforce, Zendesk, OpenAI, etc.) is gone from the homepage.
- **Customer spotlight removed**: The Johnson Controls case study quote and the "Success stories" section are no longer on the homepage.
- **Enterprise/security capabilities section removed**: Blocks covering Security Controls, 99.9+% uptime SLA, Compliance & Governance, and Scalability were removed.
- **CTA section removed**: The "Take the Next Step with Fluid Topics" call-to-action with "Get in touch" link is gone.
- These products/features still appear in the footer navigation (Agentic AI, Generative AI, etc.), so the offerings themselves haven't been discontinued — just deprioritized or moved off the homepage.
