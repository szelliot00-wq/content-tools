## What Changed
Oxygen XML's shop page has been updated to emphasize AI capabilities across their product line and clarify perpetual license terminology throughout.

## Notable Updates
- **Tab renamed:** "New Licenses" tab is now "New License & Subscriptions," signaling a broader purchase focus
- **"AI-Ready" rebranded to "AI-ready"** (lowercase) across all Enterprise tiers for XML Editor, XML Developer, XML Author, and XML Author floating licenses
- **New AI info buttons added** next to every Enterprise (AI-ready) tier: a tooltip button explaining that the license "unlocks the enterprise functionality of the Oxygen AI Positron add-on, by allowing you to connect to your preferred AI service" — and that the user's AI service account may incur additional costs
- **Perpetual license labels clarified:** All instances of "License & 1-year SMP" and "License & 2-year SMP" are now explicitly labeled "Perpetual License & 1-year SMP" and "Perpetual License & 2-year SMP" across all products (XML Editor, XML Developer, XML Author, Publishing Engine, WebHelp, PDF Chemistry)
- **Prices unchanged** across all products and tiers
