# oXygen XML Editor — Changes 2026-06-19

### Pricing

## What Changed
The monitored page completely changed from Fluid Topics' search features page to Oxygen XML's Business License shop, now displaying a full product catalog with EUR pricing — including a notable new "AI-Ready" tier branding across their Enterprise licenses and new AI-focused subscription tiers for Oxygen Feedback.

## Notable Updates
- **"Enterprise (AI-Ready)" tier introduced** — Oxygen XML rebranded their top Enterprise license tier across XML Editor, XML Developer, XML Author, and XML Author products to signal AI readiness as a differentiator.
- **Oxygen Feedback AI subscription tiers added** — Four new tiers explicitly branded around AI: Starter AI (€350/mo), Team AI (€630/mo), Business AI (€1,890/mo), and Corporate AI (contact for pricing) — all billed annually.
- **Oxygen AI Positron listed as a standalone product** — now appears in the main product catalog with its own shop entry and EULA, suggesting it has graduated to a fully commercialized offering.
- **Oxygen JSON Editor now available via subscription** — listed at €7/month billed annually (user-based), indicating a low-cost entry point for JSON-only users.
- **Oxygen Scripting** is now purchasable directly in the shop with multiple license options, previously it may have required contact sales.
- **Full self-serve pricing published** — perpetual licenses with SMP (Software Maintenance Plan) bundles for 1 and 2 years are now visible, e.g., XML Editor Enterprise user-based: €1,465 (1-yr SMP) / €1,678 (2-yr SMP); floating: €4,397 / €5,037.
