# Candice Pressinger part one

Video ID: `cTsvxFi167s`

## Summary
This video features Candice Pressinger, who is responsible for payment security, fraud, and optimization at US Bank Elevon Merchant Services Europe. She discusses her career journey, which was shaped by curiosity and a passion for data protection, leading her to the payments industry. Candice outlines her leadership style, emphasizing candor, empowerment, and creating psychologically safe environments for her teams. A central theme is her belief that security and innovation are not opposing forces but are complementary, with security acting as a growth lever.

## Key insights
- **Value-Driven Leadership:** Candice leads with candor, empowerment, and psychological safety, fostering an environment where team members feel secure to innovate, challenge ideas, and take accountability without fear of failure. This approach aims to bring forward the best collective ideas, especially from quieter voices.
- **Prioritizing Curiosity in Recruitment:** Beyond competence, Candice highly values curiosity, accountability (ownership and comfort with making mistakes), and adaptability in potential team members. She believes curiosity is fundamental for innovation, problem-solving, and thriving in rapidly changing environments.
- **Security as a Growth Lever:** A core reframe from Candice is that security and innovation are complementary, not opposites. She posits that effective security acts as a "growth lever" by reducing risk, cost, and friction, thereby optimizing outcomes, driving revenue, and helping merchants combat the innovation of fraudsters.
