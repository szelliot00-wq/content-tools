# Willem  Wellinghoff - UK Chair Ecommpay

Video ID: `DxbwKgoB9p4`

## Summary
Willem Wellinghoff, UK Chair at Ecommpay, shares his unconventional career journey from law to payments and his leadership philosophy focused on collaboration, purpose, and curiosity. He outlines Ecommpay's vision for "ultimate financial inclusivity," which involves offering diverse, trusted payment methods and improving accessibility for underserved populations, including those with disabilities. Wellinghoff also discusses key innovations like stable coins and open finance, the importance of customer-centricity, and debunks the myth of instant payments, emphasizing trust as the foundation of the industry.

## Key insights
- **Unconventional Career Path:** Wellinghoff's journey from studying law to working in-house legal/compliance, then co-founding a fintech and leading at Ecommpay, highlights the value of curiosity, grit, and not adhering to a linear career path.
- **Leadership Principles:** Effective leadership in the payments industry requires a clear vision and purpose, discipline, robust communication, and fostering a collaborative spirit that encourages teams to challenge the status quo and strive for continuous improvement.
- **Ultimate Financial Inclusivity:** Ecommpay's mission extends beyond merely offering varied payment methods to merchants; it deeply focuses on ensuring payment accessibility for all, including individuals with disabilities, recognizing the significant "Purple Pound" market.
- **Transformative Innovations:** Stable coins are highlighted as a crucial innovation for instant, traceable, global payments (seen as a currency, not just an investment), alongside open and embedded finance for building payment system sovereignty and reducing reliance on a few dominant card schemes.
- **Customer Centricity and Value:** Being customer-centric means deeply understanding evolving customer needs (whether cost-driven or strategic) and consistently delivering added value to build strong, long-term partnerships beyond just competitive pricing.
- **The Power of Curiosity and Grit:** Curiosity is essential for evolution, challenging existing norms, and understanding the 'why' behind actions. This mindset, combined with the grit to take action and persevere through challenges, is vital for personal and business growth.
- **The Myth of Instant Payments:** Contrary to consumer perception, payments are not truly instant but involve a complex, multi-step authorization and settlement process. This underlies the critical role of trust and resilience within the payments ecosystem.
- **Trust as the Core of Payments:** The entire payments industry, from technical layers to consumer confidence, is fundamentally built on trust, emphasizing the importance of strong foundations, collaboration, and clear communication.
