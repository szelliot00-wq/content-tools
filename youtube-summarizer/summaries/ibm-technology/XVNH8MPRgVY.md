# How Smart Organizations Will Use AI: Jevons Paradox & Workforce Impact

Video ID: `XVNH8MPRgVY`

## Summary
The video challenges the notion that organizations win by cutting costs and jobs, especially concerning AI. It argues that smart organizations leverage AI as an enabler for innovation, growth, and strategic advantage, rather than seeing it as a tool for workforce reduction. Through Jevons Paradox, the speaker explains that increased efficiency from AI will lower costs, leading to an explosion in demand for new services and higher-level, more complex human roles, ultimately requiring more skilled employees.

## Key insights
- Smart organizations view AI as an enabler for innovation, growth, and competitive advantage, not just a cost-cutting measure.
- Jevons Paradox illustrates that increased efficiency (like from AI) leads to lower costs, which in turn expands demand and creates new categories of work, requiring more human input overall.
- AI will shift, rather than eliminate, jobs, leading to a greater need for high-context, high-accountability roles such as problem framing, AI supervision, customer-facing positions, and cross-functional coordination.
- Successful organizations will invest in their workforce to leverage AI effectively, realizing they will need more, and smarter, employees for new opportunities.
- Essential skills for employees in the AI era include adaptability, lifelong learning, critical thinking, and creativity, as AI automates mundane tasks, freeing humans for higher-level work.
