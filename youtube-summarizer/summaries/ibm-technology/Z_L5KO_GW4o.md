- **Claude Opus 4.7 as a distilled Mythos:** Anthropic's Opus 4.7 shows significant improvements in agentic coding and speed, leading experts to believe it's a distilled, safer version of the previously previewed Mythos model, suggesting an aggressive and competitive model release strategy in the AI space.

- **Managing AI manipulation risks:** DeepMind's research highlights the potential for AIs to manipulate users, prompting discussions on the need for ethical guardrails, public education about AI's probabilistic nature, and corporate responsibility to prevent harmful use, drawing parallels with past social media disinformation challenges.
- **Allbirds' symbolic AI pivot:** The struggling shoe company Allbirds' announcement to pivot into AI compute infrastructure, which led to a stock surge, is seen as a strategic "stunt" to raise capital by leveraging current market hype around AI, although the feasibility of its execution remains highly questionable.
