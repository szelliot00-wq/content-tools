## Key insights
- **Core problem solved:** Landing in a foreign country without internet access makes phones nearly useless — no maps, ride apps, hotel info, or messaging. Saily addresses this before departure.
- **Works in 200+ destinations:** Users can search by country (e.g., Turkey, USA, Thailand) and browse available data plans directly in the app.
- **eSIM installation is app-guided:** No physical SIM card or mobile shop visit required — the entire setup happens in-app before travel begins.
- **Prepaid, no surprise bills:** Plans are purchased upfront with a known cost, eliminating the risk of unexpected roaming charges.
- **Data tracking widget:** A home screen widget lets users monitor remaining data without opening the app — useful since navigation, video calls, and background apps drain data quickly.
- **Rewards/credits system:** Users earn points through plan purchases and referrals, redeemable against future plans or travel perks like lounge access and Uber vouchers.
- **Saily Ultra tier:** A premium plan for heavy travelers that bundles unlimited international data, credits, a NordVPN subscription, lounge access, and a fast track airport pass.
- **24/7 live chat support:** Designed to address connectivity issues in real time, regardless of time zones.
- **Business solutions:** eSIM plans available for corporate employee fleets, accessible via the app's profile section.
- **Security focus:** Inherited from NordVPN's parent company — includes blocking of malicious sites, trackers, and ads during browsing.
- **Discount code:** "Daniel Tech" gives 15% off at checkout — standard affiliate/creator promotion.

## Use cases
- Travelers who want mobile data ready the moment they land, without airport SIM card hunting.
- Digital nomads working remotely across multiple countries who need reliable, trackable data.
- Business travelers managing connectivity for themselves or a team across international trips.
- Frequent flyers who hop between countries and want a single subscription (Ultra) rather than repeated one-off purchases.
- Budget-conscious travelers who want predictable, upfront data costs with no roaming bill surprises.
- Travelers who value added perks (lounge access, ride-share vouchers) bundled with their connectivity plan.
- Companies needing to provision mobile data for employees traveling internationally.

## Patterns & frameworks
**Pre-trip preparation model:** The core UX pattern Saily is built around — research, purchase, and install your data plan *before* departure rather than scrambling after landing. This flips the traditional "figure it out at the airport" approach into a pre-packaged, zero-stress workflow.

**Tiered plan structure:** Standard per-country or regional prepaid plans for casual travelers vs. Saily Ultra for power users. This is a classic freemium/tiered SaaS pattern applied to travel connectivity — entry-level access with an upsell to an all-inclusive subscription.

**Rewards flywheel:** Purchase plans → earn credits → redeem for discounts or perks → encourages repeat purchases and referrals. A standard loyalty loop designed to increase retention and organic growth.

**Bundled ecosystem expansion:** Saily is moving beyond pure connectivity (data) into a broader travel utility platform (lounge access, ride vouchers, VPN, fast track passes, business plans). This follows the "land and expand" product strategy — hook users with one core utility, then broaden the value proposition to increase stickiness.
