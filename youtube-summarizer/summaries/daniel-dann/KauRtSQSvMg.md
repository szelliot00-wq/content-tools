## Patterns & frameworks
**Prepare-before-you-land workflow**: The video's central framework is shifting connectivity setup from a reactive airport task to a proactive pre-trip step. The pattern is: download app → select destination → choose plan → install eSIM → travel → activate on arrival. This eliminates the stressful "no internet at the airport" problem entirely.

**Tiered product structure**: Saily is presented in three tiers — standard plans (pay-per-trip), Saily Ultra (subscription for frequent travelers), and Business (team dashboard). Each tier targets a distinct user segment with escalating features and value.

**Credits-as-loyalty loop**: A repeatable pattern where purchasing plans earns credits, credits reduce the cost of future plans, and referrals generate additional credits. This incentivizes repeat usage and word-of-mouth without traditional discount mechanics.

**Bundled connectivity + security**: Rather than positioning the app as a pure data product, Saily bundles connectivity with security (tracker/ad/malicious site blocking), framing travel data as a privacy concern as much as a logistics one — a pattern borrowed from the NordVPN parent brand.
