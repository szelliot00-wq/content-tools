## Patterns & frameworks
*   **Zero-Trust Architecture**: A security model applied by PrivateAlps where no user, device, or application is trusted by default, regardless of whether they are inside or outside the network perimeter. This means even the PrivateAlps team cannot access client data, ensuring maximum privacy and security.
*   **Modular & Optional Configuration**: A design pattern for system configuration where resources (such as IPs, disk space, and CPU cores) and services can be added or removed independently based on specific workload needs. This allows users to scale their infrastructure precisely and only pay for what they actually use.
