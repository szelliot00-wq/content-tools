# Use.AI Review - 2026 | Testing 20+ AI Models in ONE Dashboard

Video ID: `3VS2Dh9UH-I`

## Summary
This video reviews Use.AI, an all-in-one platform designed to combat "app fatigue" by integrating over 20 major AI models like GPT, Claude, and Gemini into a single, easy-to-use dashboard. The core argument is that Use.AI simplifies the process of accessing, comparing, and leveraging multiple AI tools for a variety of tasks, all under one roof and for one price. It aims to boost productivity and flexibility for users by allowing seamless switching between models and enabling direct side-by-side comparisons of their outputs. The platform is most relevant to writers, researchers, developers, creators, and anyone who regularly relies on artificial intelligence and seeks to streamline their workflow.

## Key insights
-   **Problem Solved**: Use.AI addresses "app fatigue" from juggling many AI apps and subscriptions by centralizing access to numerous models.
-   **Multimodel Access**: The platform offers seamless access to over 20 "powerhouse models," including OpenAI (GPT), Anthropic (Claude Opus 4.5), Google (Gemini 3 Pro), Llama 3.3, Deepseek, and Kimik K2.
-   **Key Advantages**: Users benefit from instant switching between AI models without reloads, a clean and distraction-free interface, and the ability to compare how different models tackle the same problem, offering new perspectives for brainstorming and troubleshooting.
-   **Interface Features**: The intuitive interface includes a navigation panel for previous chats (searchable), project creation for organization, account settings, dark/light mode options, a drop-down menu for model selection, conversation starters, and the ability to attach files, generate images, or turn on web search.
-   **Voice Input**: A notable feature is the option to dictate prompts instead of typing, described as a "cheat code" for convenience.
-   **Text Generation Test (Financial Task)**:
    -   **Task**: Add numbers, spot a mistake in monthly expenses, and explain the error.
    -   **Claude Opus 4.5**: Correctly calculated the total and flagged the sum as 10 points short, identifying rent (850) as an error due to it being "very low" (an assumption without specified currency).
    -   **GPT-4 Mini**: Flagged the total discrepancy but vaguely suggested lowering groceries with no explanation, deemed a "guess."
    -   **Llama 3.3**: Calculated the total, stated the sum was wrong, but did not suggest changing any items, which the narrator considered the "most logical and correct answer."
-   **Image Generation Test ("castle in the sky")**:
    -   **Llama**: Produced a "pretty cartoonish" image with "strong Disney castle vibes."
    -   **Deepseek**: Delivered a similar image but with a "darker, cloudier, and more dramatic" mood.
    -   **Kimik K2**: Generated a "brighter, more cheerful, and super pleasant" image. The narrator refrained from picking a clear winner, inviting viewer comments.
-   **Pricing**: Use.AI offers a trial for less than $1 to test most features. Paid plans are available monthly or annually, with pricing varying based on usage and tier, allowing users to commit only when it fits their workflow.

## Patterns & frameworks
-   **Side-by-Side Model Comparison (or "Model Battle")**: This is the central framework demonstrated.
    -   **Description**: The user inputs the exact same prompt or task into multiple, distinct AI models (e.g., Claude, GPT, Llama for text; Llama, Deepseek, Kimik K2 for images) within the Use.AI dashboard.
    -   **How it works**: By having all outputs presented in one interface, users can directly compare the quality, logic, creativity, and effectiveness of each model's response for a given problem. This allows for informed decision-making on which AI model is best suited for a specific task, leading to increased productivity and better outcomes without the friction of switching between separate platforms.
