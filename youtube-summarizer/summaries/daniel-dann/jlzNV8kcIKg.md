## Key insights
- **The core problem reframe:** The video opens by arguing that creators have a "clipping problem," not a content problem — hours of usable footage go to waste because the editing process is too slow and tedious.
- **Upload flexibility:** WayinVideo accepts video uploads via drag-and-drop, file selection, or Google Drive, and supports all common video formats with no conversion required.
- **AI clipping is the flagship feature:** Rather than manually scrubbing through footage, the AI analyzes the entire video and extracts the highest-value moments automatically.
- **Configurable but low-friction setup:** Users can adjust language and clip length settings before processing, or leave everything on auto for the fastest result — designed to minimize time spent on configuration.
- **Viral score ranking:** Extracted clips are ranked by a "viral score" that predicts how likely each clip is to perform well, removing the guesswork from content selection.
- **Instant preview:** All generated clips can be previewed immediately on the same page without navigating away, enabling rapid evaluation of options.
- **Format reframing:** The editor supports switching clip aspect ratios (e.g., original to 9:16 for Reels/TikTok) with an Auto Reframe option that handles repositioning automatically.
- **Auto-generated captions:** Clicking the subtitles button instantly generates captions synced to the audio; text, timing, and visual style can all be edited within the same interface.
- **Keyword search within video:** A search feature lets users type keywords to instantly locate relevant moments inside a long video, avoiding the need to rewatch footage to find a specific segment.
- **Real-time analysis updates:** As the system processes a search query or clip request, results update in real time rather than requiring a full re-run.
- **One-platform workflow:** The entire process — clipping, reframing, captioning, and export — happens inside a single workspace with no tool-switching required.
- **Direct social publishing:** The export step includes an option to publish directly to social platforms in addition to downloading the final file.
- **Free trial offer:** New users get 200 free credits when signing up via the host's referral link, mentioned at the end of the video.

## Patterns & frameworks
**The Upload-Analyze-Select-Refine-Export Loop**
A five-step repeatable workflow the tool is built around: (1) upload or link a video, (2) let the AI analyze and extract clips, (3) review clips ranked by viral score and select the best ones, (4) refine in the editor (reframe, add captions), (5) export or publish directly. Each step is designed to minimize decision fatigue and eliminate manual searching.

**Viral Score Ranking**
A built-in prioritization model that scores each extracted clip by predicted performance. Rather than evaluating all clips equally, this surfaces the strongest moments first, functioning as an opinionated filter that guides content selection without requiring editorial judgment from the user.

**Auto Reframe**
A format-adaptation pattern where the system automatically repositions and crops video content to fit a target aspect ratio (e.g., 16:9 → 9:16). Eliminates the manual work of rebuilding clips for different platforms.

**Keyword-Driven Moment Search**
A retrieval pattern that treats a long video like a searchable document — users enter keywords and the system returns timestamped, relevant moments. This inverts the traditional workflow of linear scrubbing and makes long-form content navigable on demand.

**Single-Workspace Continuity**
A UX design pattern where every step of the workflow (clipping, editing, captioning, exporting) exists within one interface. The explicit goal is to eliminate context-switching between tools, keeping momentum and reducing friction throughout the content production process.
