## Patterns & frameworks
- **In-editor optimization loop**: The core repeatable pattern is write → analyze → adjust → preview → publish, all within a single interface. Yoast eliminates the need to move between tools by embedding every optimization step into the WordPress editor, making the process iterative and visible in real time.
- **Score-based feedback model**: Yoast uses a scoring system that responds dynamically to edits. This gamified feedback loop encourages incremental improvements — users can see immediately whether a change helps or hurts their score, reinforcing good optimization habits without requiring prior SEO knowledge.
- **Layered content optimization**: Yoast structures optimization across three distinct layers — (1) SEO/keyword relevance, (2) technical meta elements (title, slug, description), and (3) readability for human audiences. Each layer is addressed separately but contributes to an overall content quality score, ensuring neither readers nor search engines are neglected.
- **Preview-before-publish model**: Before going live, creators can simulate how their content appears in Google search results on both mobile and desktop. This pattern shifts quality control earlier in the workflow, reducing the need for post-publication fixes.
