## Summary
This video provides a detailed review of Saily eSIM, a global mobile data service developed by the team behind NordVPN, designed to provide seamless internet access for international travelers. It addresses the common frustrations of landing without internet, high roaming costs, and the inconvenience of obtaining local SIM cards by allowing users to purchase and install digital data plans for over 200 countries and regions directly from an app before their trip. The reviewer demonstrates the straightforward user experience, highlights transparent pricing, a loyalty credit system, and built-in privacy features, positioning Saily as a practical solution for casual, frequent, and business travelers, as well as digital nomads.

## Key insights
-   **Problem Solved:** Saily addresses the issues of travelers landing without internet, exorbitant international roaming charges (e.g., $10-15 per day), airport Wi-Fi requiring local phone numbers, and the time-consuming process of purchasing physical local SIM cards abroad (dealing with stores, paperwork, and language barriers).
-   **Product Overview:** Saily is a mobile application available on both iOS and Android, enabling users to purchase and manage eSIM data plans digitally for more than 200 countries and regions worldwide. It is developed by the creators of NordVPN.
-   **Ease of Use & Installation:** The entire process happens digitally within the app. Users download the app, select and purchase a data plan, and install the eSIM *before* traveling. Upon landing in the destination country, the phone automatically connects to a local network, bypassing the need for physical SIMs or airport purchases. The installation process is described as taking approximately 2 minutes.
-   **Flexible Plan Options:** The app categorizes plans into "country" (for single destinations), "regional" (for multi-country travel, e.g., a Europe trip), and "ultra" options. Data plans range from 1GB to 20GB, with unlimited plans also available, and each option clearly displays its validity period.
-   **Transparent Pricing:** The order summary page clearly shows all details: selected country, data allowance, validity duration, and total price, ensuring no hidden fees or unexpected charges.
-   **Discount & Savings:** Users can apply a coupon code (e.g., "Daniel Tech") during checkout for an instant 15% discount, which is reflected immediately on the screen before purchase.
-   **Loyalty Program:** Saily features a built-in credit system where every plan purchase earns credits that can be applied towards future purchases. This system rewards frequent travelers, with some users potentially getting every third or fourth plan "basically for free."
-   **Privacy & Security:** Benefiting from its NordVPN lineage, Saily offers optional web protection and an ad blocker within the app. The ad blocker can reduce mobile data usage by up to 28% by preventing browser ads and background scripts from consuming data.
-   **Customer Support & Refund:** Saily provides 24/7 chat support for any issues. Additionally, they offer a full refund if a user's device is found to be incompatible with eSIM technology, "no questions asked."

## Use cases
-   **Casual travelers:** Individuals who travel abroad once or twice a year and need reliable internet for navigation (Google Maps), sharing photos, and messaging family, without incurring high daily roaming charges from their home carrier.
-   **Frequent travelers:** People who fly multiple times a year and can benefit significantly from the financial savings, loyalty credits, and the convenience of instant connectivity upon arrival without the hassle of seeking out local SIM card shops.
-   **Business travelers / Work travelers:** Professionals who require immediate and uninterrupted internet access upon landing to check emails, join virtual meetings, and upload files, ensuring productivity from the moment they arrive.
-   **Digital nomads and remote workers:** Individuals who work online and rely on constant internet connectivity, benefiting from the ability to stay productive and connected without needing to search for Wi-Fi hotspots or deal with local SIM card acquisition.
-   **Anyone leaving their home country:** A general solution for ensuring seamless internet access globally, avoiding common travel connectivity frustrations, and simplifying the process of staying connected.
