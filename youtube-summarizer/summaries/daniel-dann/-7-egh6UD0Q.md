# Fireflies AI vs Fellow App - 2026 | Which One Is More Accurate and Smarter?

Video ID: `-7-egh6UD0Q`

## Summary
This video argues that Fellow is a superior alternative to Fireflies AI, especially for executives and team leaders. The core argument is that Fellow is a comprehensive meeting assistant that supports the entire meeting lifecycle—before, during, and after—while Fireflies primarily focuses on transcription. The video details Fellow's unique features, such as AI-powered pre-meeting briefs, real-time recording controls, and advanced post-meeting intelligence, positioning it as the better choice for teams that need to drive work forward from their meetings.

## Key insights
- **Pre-Meeting Preparation:** Fellow provides collaborative meeting agendas, hundreds of templates, and AI-powered pre-meeting briefs that summarize previous discussions and action items. The video explicitly states Fireflies has no pre-meeting features like agendas or AI briefs.
- **During-Meeting Controls:** Fellow offers a one-click "pause" function to stop recording when confidential topics arise and a "redact" feature to permanently remove sensitive sections (audio, video, and transcript) after the meeting. Fireflies records nonstop, requiring users to edit or delete sensitive content afterward.
- **Recording Flexibility:** Fellow offers both bot and botless recording options across Zoom, Teams, Google Meet, and Slack Huddles. In contrast, Fireflies' botless option is limited to a Chrome extension that only works on Google Meet.
- **Post-Meeting Intelligence ("Ask Fellow"):** Fellow's "Ask Fellow" feature functions like an "AI chief of staff" that can search a user's entire meeting history and surface specific answers with timestamps. It can query across specific channels, teams, or subsets of meetings, providing organizational intelligence that Fireflies' "Ask Fred" lacks.
- **Task Management and Follow-up:** Fellow automatically generates AI summaries, syncs action items to tools like Jira, Asana, ClickUp, Salesforce, and HubSpot, and can draft follow-up emails based on meeting commitments.
- **Enterprise-Grade Governance and Privacy:** Fellow allows workspace admins to set organization-wide recording rules (e.g., restricting who can record or preventing certain meetings from being recorded). It is SOC 2 Type 2 certified, HIPAA and GDPR compliant, and never trains AI models on customer data, making it suitable for regulated industries like healthcare, finance, and legal.
- **Accuracy and Language Support:** The video claims Fellow has over 95% transcription accuracy across 92 languages.
- **Target Audience and Pricing:** The video positions Fellow as being built for teams needing to drive work forward, while Fireflies is for individuals who need simple transcription. It also states that Fellow is priced lower than Fireflies at every tier while offering more features.

## Use cases
- **Executives and Team Leaders:** For leaders in back-to-back meetings who need to ensure every meeting is productive and drives action.
- **Recurring Team Meetings:** Using AI pre-meeting briefs to summarize previous discussions and outstanding action items, so the team can start aligned without wasting time on "where did we leave off?"
- **Meetings with Sensitive Information:** Using the one-click pause and post-meeting redaction features to handle confidential topics in legal, financial, or HR-related discussions.
- **Cross-Functional Project Management:** Automatically syncing action items and decisions from meetings directly into project management tools like Asana, Jira, or Linear to ensure accountability and follow-through.
- **Client and Sales Calls:** Using "Ask Fellow" to quickly recall past commitments (e.g., "What did we commit to with this client last quarter?") and using the platform to sync notes and tasks into CRMs like Salesforce or HubSpot.
- **Regulated Industries:** For organizations in healthcare, finance, or legal fields that require tools with strong security and compliance, such as SOC 2, HIPAA, and GDPR certifications, and guarantees that data is not used for AI model training.

## Patterns & frameworks
- **The Full Meeting Lifecycle:** This is the core framework the video uses to structure its argument for Fellow's superiority. It breaks the meeting process into three distinct phases and highlights how Fellow provides support for each one:
    - **Before the meeting:** Focuses on preparation and alignment through features like collaborative agendas, templates, and AI-powered pre-meeting briefs. The goal is to ensure everyone arrives prepared and on the same page.
    - **During the meeting:** Emphasizes real-time support and control. This includes real-time transcription, note-taking, and unique controls like the ability to pause and resume recording to manage sensitive information.
    - **After the meeting:** Centers on turning discussion into action. This phase includes AI-generated summaries, automatic task syncing with other tools, drafting follow-up emails, and providing a cross-meeting intelligence layer with "Ask Fellow."
