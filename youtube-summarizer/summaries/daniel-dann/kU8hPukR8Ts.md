## Patterns & frameworks

**Complaint-to-Gap Analysis**
Start with existing customer complaints in a target niche rather than with product ideas. The process: identify the highest-volume pain points (e.g., material quality at ~33% of complaints), then map those to unaddressed product attributes. This surfaces differentiation opportunities grounded in real buyer frustration rather than assumption.

**Competitive Positioning Funnel**
When a niche is classified as highly competitive, the platform shifts the output from "launch this product" to "find a defensible sub-niche." The pattern: broad niche → competitive density assessment → narrowed target segment (e.g., pet hair + apartment owners) → specialized product brief.

**Multimodal IP Risk Workflow**
A three-input analysis loop: (1) text-based product concept and technical specs, (2) uploaded product images for visual design comparison, (3) target legal jurisdictions. Outputs separate risk scores for **industrial design patents** (visual similarity) and **utility patents** (functional/technological overlap), plus actionable redesign guidance.

**Specialist Role Switching**
The platform supports shifting between a general-purpose chat agent and domain-specific "specialist roles" (e.g., IP Risk Specialist) within the same session. The specialist roles carry pre-loaded context from prior steps, enabling a handoff from market research to legal analysis without re-entering background information.
