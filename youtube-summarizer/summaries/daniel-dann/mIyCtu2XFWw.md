## Summary

The video focuses specifically on HeyGen's translation feature, which converts existing videos into other languages while preserving the creator's voice and syncing lip movements to the new language. ## Patterns & frameworks

**Create Once, Reuse Smarter**
The central mental model of the video. Rather than treating each video as a standalone production, you record or upload a single source video and distribute it across audiences, languages, and contexts using AI tools. Reduces per-video production cost and time.

**Layered Complexity (Basic → Advanced Mode)**
HeyGen follows a progressive disclosure pattern: the default workflow gets you a usable result quickly, while an advanced mode unlocks granular controls (lip sync tuning, noise removal, captions, glossary). This lets beginners get value immediately while power users retain control.

**Digital Avatar as Reusable Asset**
Instead of treating yourself as something you must physically deploy for every video, HeyGen frames your likeness and voice as a reusable digital asset — created once, deployed repeatedly across content without additional recording sessions.
