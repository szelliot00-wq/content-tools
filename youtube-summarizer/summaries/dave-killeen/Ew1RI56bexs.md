# Speech to text 200 WPM! Ditch the keyboard! Superwhisper.com + Flowvoice.ai use cases.

Video ID: `Ew1RI56bexs`

## Summary
The video introduces Superwhisper.com and Flowvoice.ai, two speech-to-text tools aimed at saving time and boosting productivity by replacing traditional keyboard typing. It demonstrates how these applications enable users to quickly dictate text, with AI assisting in real-time corrections, reformatting, and styling for both short, structured thoughts and longer, more complex content. The presenter highlights the ability to achieve a deeper state of "flow" and significant time savings by efficiently converting spoken words into polished text.

## Key insights
- **Flowvoice.ai** provides a simple, rapid solution for transcribing well-structured text directly from speech, allowing for immediate voice-activated corrections and stylistic transformations (e.g., changing text to a "Steve Jobs style").
- **Superwhisper.com** offers advanced functionality through customizable "modes" (e.g., email, meeting notes, ideation) that guide AI in formatting and structuring voice input for various tasks, with options to choose different voice and language models balancing speed and accuracy.
- Both tools dramatically increase content generation speed compared to typing, with Superwhisper further extending utility through an iOS keyboard for on-the-go ideation and content creation.
- The integration of AI goes beyond simple transcription, enabling intelligent manipulation, reformatting, and restructuring of spoken content based on specific prompts, which is claimed to save users a substantial amount of time, potentially "one day a week."
