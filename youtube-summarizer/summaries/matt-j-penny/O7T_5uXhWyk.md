# NEW Claude Visualiser Is Replacing Entire Startups

Video ID: `O7T_5uXhWyk`

## Summary
This video introduces Claude's new "Visualizer" feature, an inline chat tool that generates interactive charts, diagrams, and visual elements directly within the conversation. The presenter argues that this capability renders many niche online tools and startups useless, as users no longer need to visit external websites for functions like compound interest calculators or brand color planners. The video demonstrates its use for financial planning, brand design, and educational purposes, highlighting AI's shift towards multimodal, integrated assistance that proactively helps users. It's relevant for business owners looking to save time, increase productivity, and understand the evolving landscape of AI-driven business erosion.

## Key insights
-   **Claude Visualizer:** A new feature within Claude chat that allows the AI to automatically or upon request generate interactive elements like charts, diagrams, timelines, and data visualizations directly inline within the chat interface. This is distinct from previous "Artifacts" which were separate from the chat.
-   **Disruption of Niche Startups:** The Visualizer poses a significant threat to businesses that offer single-purpose online tools (e.g., compound interest calculators, color planners) as their primary function or lead magnet. Users can now replicate these tools' functionality with a single prompt in Claude.
-   **Demonstrated Examples:**
    -   **Compound Interest Calculator:** Quickly calculates future values based on initial deposit (e.g., £50,000), monthly contributions (e.g., £1,500), annual interest rate (e.g., 8%), and compounding period (e.g., monthly over 35 years, resulting in £4.2 million).
    -   **Brand Color Planner:** Generates complementary color palettes (e.g., warm, cool, neutral, purple shades) along with hex codes, replicating functionality of dedicated design tools.
    -   **Interactive Periodic Table:** Visualizes elements with details like electrons in each shell, offering an educational and more visceral learning experience than text-based explanations.
-   **Visualizer vs. Artifacts:** Visualizer creates elements for "here and now" use within the chat and is not primarily designed for export, whereas Artifacts are permanent, exportable HTML assets meant for external embedding (though Visualizer code could potentially be requested).
-   **Broader Implications of AI Evolution:** The feature demonstrates AI providers eroding existing businesses and shows AI becoming more multimodal, integrated into personal and work lives, moving beyond purely text-based interactions to offer a wider range of elements for problem-solving and understanding.
-   **AI's Smarter Assistance:** The presenter believes this is a step towards AI knowing how to help users better, reducing the need for users to know how to specifically prompt or use AI.
-   **Call to Action:** The video concludes by promoting a free list of over 600 Claude code skills for saving time and increasing productivity in business.

## Use cases
-   **Financial Planning:** Quickly calculating compound interest, savings goals, loan repayments, or investment growth without needing to visit external financial calculators.
-   **Brand & Design Work:** Generating harmonious color palettes for new brands, logos, or marketing materials, complete with hex codes for easy integration into design software.
-   **Education & Learning:** Visualizing complex scientific concepts (e.g., periodic table, chemical structures, biological processes), historical timelines, or geographical data for better understanding and interactive study.
-   **Business Analysis:** Creating simple charts, diagrams, or timelines to visualize data, project plans, or processes directly within a chat for quick insights and internal discussions.
-   **Personal Productivity:** Streamlining tasks that previously required switching between multiple niche online tools, centralizing work within the Claude interface.
-   **Rapid Prototyping/Ideation:** Quickly generating visual representations of ideas or concepts for quick feedback or exploration without the need for dedicated software.

## Patterns & frameworks
-   **AI-driven Business Erosion:** The video highlights a pattern where advanced AI features, like Claude's Visualizer, increasingly absorb the functionality of niche single-purpose online tools and startups. This creates significant competitive pressure, potentially rendering many existing businesses obsolete by integrating their core offerings directly into AI platforms.
-   **Multimodal AI Integration:** A recurring pattern observed is AI evolving beyond purely text-based interactions to incorporate diverse interactive and visual elements (charts, diagrams, calculators). This indicates a shift towards AI becoming more integrated into daily tasks and personal/professional workflows, providing a richer, more intuitive user experience.
-   **"Here and Now" Problem Solving:** The Visualizer exemplifies a pattern of AI assisting users with immediate, in-context problem-solving. Unlike generating permanent assets, its primary function is to provide quick, temporary tools and visualizations that aid understanding or decision-making at the moment of interaction.
